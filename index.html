(ᯤ) My Top 3 Spotify Artist and Songs

Taylor Swift

Taylor Alison Swift is an American singer-songwriter. Known for her autobiographical songwriting, artistic versatility, and cultural impact, Swift is one of the best-selling music artists in history, with estimated sales of over 200 million records. She has also been my favorite singer since 2022, and my favorite album of hers is reputation (see album below). Also, she is now engaged to Travis Kelce!

  • Enchanted by Taylor Swift
  • Dress by Taylor Swift
  • Fearless by Taylor Swift

KATSEYE

Katseye is a six-member global girl group formed by HYBE and Geffen Records through the reality series Pop Star Academy: KATSEYE. The members are Sophia from the Philippines, Manon from Switzerland (with Ghanaian and Swiss-Italian roots), Yoonchae from Korea, Daniela (Cuban-Venezuelan), Lara (Indian-American), and Megan (Chinese-Singaporean). My favorite album is Beautiful Chaos, and I’ve been stanning them since 2024. My biases are Sophia, Daniela, and Manon. I like Sophia because she’s Filipina, and I also like Daniela and Manon because they also have curly hair like mine!

  • Gabriela by KATSEYE
  • Touch by KATSEYE
  • Gnarly by KATSEYE

HUNTR/X / Saja Boys (from KPop Demon Hunters OST)

HUNTR/X is a three-member girl group from the 2025 Netflix film KPop Demon Hunters. The members are Rumi (Arden Cho / EJAE), Mira (May Hong / Audrey Nuna), and Zoey (Ji-young Yoo / Rei Ami). Saja Boys is the boy group in KPop Demon Hunters, with Jinu (Ahn Hyo-seop / Andrew Choi) and members voiced by Neckwav, Danny Chung, Kevin Woo, and SamUIL Lee. The soundtrack also features TWICE’s Jeongyeon, Jihyo, and Chaeyoung. If you wanna see their soundtracks, check the album below!

  • Free by Kpop Demon Hunters (Rumi and Jinu)
  • Your Idol by Kpop Demon Hunters (Saja Boys)
  • What it Sounds Like by Kpop Demon Hunters (HUNTR/X)
